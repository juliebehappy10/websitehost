Label what matters for navigation.

The highest-value labels are not generic object tags. They capture passability, dynamic obstacles, route decisions, and the reason a human changes course.

Level 1 - Route metadata Scene type, start, goal, success/failure, route quality, lighting, and sensor quality.
Level 2 - Navigation semantics Free space, doors, corridors, key obstacles, ground surfaces, human traffic, and semantic zones.
Level 3 - Spatial structure 2.5D distance, footprints, 3D boxes for key objects, occupancy maps, and dynamic obstacle trajectories.
Level 4 - Behavior decisions Stops, gaze shifts, yielding, waiting, route re-plans, and the cause of each navigation decision.
